How To Play - Life Simulator

Alita is recently divorced. Unfortunately, her ex-spouse and business partner had a gambling problem. All business and personal savings were used to payoff his debt. Alita now has no job and no money; however, a close friend is providing her with a free room for a limited amount of time. Help Alita get back on her feet and live her best life!

The goal of the game is to help Alita become a Developer in 30 Weeks.

Alita will need work experience and education to achieve her goal. She can use her phone to start various activities that will affect her wealth, health, happiness, social, and education.

You have 16 hours in each day. Each day will end at 11pm.
